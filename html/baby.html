FAQ'S

When to start oil massage for a newborn baby?

Experts recommend starting an oil massage after waiting for ten days or two weeks after the birth.

Is baby oil safe to use on babies? (Is Mamaearth baby oil safe?)

Yes. Mamaearth baby oil is safe for babies because it is dermatologically tested. It does not contain any harmful chemicals or abrasives.

What Mamaearth oil to use for baby massage?

Mamaearth Soothing Massage Oil for Babies is best for massaging your baby regularly.

Can adults use baby oil?

Yes. Although the oil is specially formulated for babies, it is mild and gentle and can thus be used by adults of any age.
